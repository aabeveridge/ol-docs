Activity-Specific Data Engineering

We engineer customized software workflows and dedicated APIs for specific recreational activities. Below are the activities we currently service:

Shooting and Hunters

Fishing and Anglers

Outdoor Fitness and Hikers

Services

We partner with conservation researchers, consumer analysts, and marketing experts to engineer new analytics tools and to build new data-driven applications.

Dedicated APIs

We develop dedicated APIs that aggregate diverse data sources related to outdoor recreation and conservation.

Analytics Development

We produce customized dashboards and new web applications based on the individual analytics needs of our clients.

Internet of Things

We solve remote data collection problems by providing customized monitoring workflows for a wide range of sensor data.
